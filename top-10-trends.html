Top 10 Trends in Computer Vision

Computer Vision had an extended way to attain the position it has today in the industry. Commencing as an ambition to furnish a camera with mortal intellects, CV has amassed a generous amount of recognition and investments.

With the world's biggest market, China alone has invested a whopping number of $3.9 billion in this boon of technology. This article will scrutinize the top upcoming trends in CV that can be swiveled into a vast temptation in the future of AI:-



  1. Automatic image annotation

    From finding the person tagged in a post on Instagram to finding the desired aesthetic on Pinterest, this application of CV present in these processes help in analyzing every visual and characterized based on its features, in the form of different keywords related to it. This allows users to get the closest result related to the input they give in the search bar.


  2. Self-regulated Understanding

    With most of the enterprises and activities focusing on bringing more involvement of digitalization especially in the pandemic, people are getting more involved in learning by themselves. According to the curriculum assigned to the learner, this allows CV to optimize their interests based on the subjects and topic they wish to work over to.


  3. Visual Search

    Visual Search allows its users to browse more information regarding their desired topic or product, purely based on its image or advertisement, in a fraction of minute. A survey by Businesswire reveals that around 62% of gen z and millennials choose to use image search over search bar. Renowned search engines and shopping websites like Amazon and Google are already adopting this development.




  4. OCR technology

    Optical Character Recognition or Optical Character Reader enables the recordkeepers to digitalize varied visuals, text formats, or various other contents to encourage any sort of alterations or penning it down anywhere or as many times one wishes to. The reduces hours that are consumed by manual data collection and extracts the desired piece of information shortly.




  5. Point cloud

    Point cloud is a series of carefully aligned objects and spaces along with a 3D coordinate system in the form of points, where they represent positions of X, Y, and Z . This new trend encourages the smooth functioning of building 3D impressions for animations or drafting projects, and also in tracking objects, recognizing, sorting and differentiating images.


  6. Deep learning

    As the name suggests, deep learning is the processing of Computer Vision step-by-step. A very popular deep learning model known as CNN (Convolutional Neural Networks) is one of the most CV applications present today, that cuts an image into layers of distinguishing features like edges, colors, etc., analyzing each other and processing the result with other existing similar images.


  7. Edge Computing

    Edge Computing. This qualifies machines like MRI scanners, large gas turbines to function and modify themselves exclusively based on their ordeals on their own.


  8. VR

    VR or Virtual Reality is yet another result of the advancement of CV which permits its users to experience a live 3D atmosphere, which is purely synthetic and can control the simulations of activities they see happening around them, according to their preferences. VR these days is primarily distinguished among the gaming community.




  9. Thermal Imaging Analysis

    Terror of global warming and increase in temperature at an alarming rate is encouraging scientists to adopt this latest application of CV, which generates the visuals of infrared radiation of variation in temperatures. Notifying with a skeptical shift in weather or temperature is yet one of the qualities of this operation.


  10. Video Analytics Solution

    A conventional surveillance network is a significant virtue of any industry or security department that wishes to ensure safety and privacy. This is when Video Analytics appears in handy. This system authorizes the organization to scan and recognize the components and figures present in the video and gives precise details of their movements.
